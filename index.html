About Me

Hello my name is Paweł. I am living and working in Gdańsk. From the 4th grade of elementary school I played table tennis at the AZS AWFiS Gdańsk club. My biggest success was winning the junior team championship of the Pomeranian Voivodeship in 2005. I'm interested in politics and sport. In my free time I play board and team games or surf the internet. I took part in the "Harpagan" orientation rally several times.
In 2018, I finished the 6-month "Web Developer" bootcamp which started my development career. Every day I try to learn something new and follow the highest front-end standards. Currently, I work as a freelancer while deepening my knowledge towards a full-stack developer. I encourage you to look at some of my projects and repositories.
